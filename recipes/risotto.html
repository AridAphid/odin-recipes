Pea & Mint Risotto

Description

Ingredients
  • olive oil
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1l chicken stock
  • 250g risotto rice
  • 60g peas
  • 20g mint
Steps
  1. fry the onions and garilic in the oil
  2. once golden, add in the rice and spoon around until the riec has turned see through
  3. add the chicken stock in slowly 250ml at a time
  4. allow to simmer for 10 mins, add in the peas and simmer further 5mins
  5. once creamy, take off the flame and add mint to garnish
